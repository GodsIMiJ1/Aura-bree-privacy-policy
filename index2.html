About AURA-BREE

AURA-BREE is your personal therapeutic AI companion. The app offers a calm, judgment free space to check in with yourself, get guidance, and build resilience. Everything is designed for privacy, clarity, and control.

Wellness note: AURA-BREE supports wellbeing but is not a medical device and does not provide professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Mood tracking

Log mood and notes, then review trends in Progress.

AI chat support

Talk through what you are feeling. Conversations are processed only to provide responses.

Tarot card reader

Draw cards for Past, Present, and Future. Use insights to reflect and reset.

Daily horoscope

Receive a brief outlook to set your vibe for the day.

Dream interpreter

Log a dream and explore symbolic meanings for personal insight.

Emergency toolkit

Quick access to crisis numbers and a guided 4-4-6 breathing pattern for grounding.

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how GodsIMiJ AI Solutions handles information in the AURA-BREE Android application.

1. Information we collect

  • User provided content - mood entries, notes, chat messages, tarot selections, dream logs, and horoscope preferences that you choose to enter.
  • Device and diagnostics - device model, operating system version, app version, and performance diagnostics such as crashes and errors.
  • Usage data - anonymous feature usage counts that help us understand which parts of the app are helpful.

We do not request your name, email, or phone number unless you contact us directly for support.

2. How we use information

  • Provide core features including chat responses, mood tracking, tarot reader, horoscope, and dream interpretation.
  • Maintain security, debug problems, and improve reliability and accessibility.
  • Respond to support requests that you initiate.

3. AI processing

When you use AI features, the text you enter is sent to an AI processing service provider that acts as our data processor. The provider uses your input only to return a response and to protect the service from abuse. We do not use your content to build advertising profiles.

Prompts are sent over HTTPS. Service providers are required to handle data under confidentiality and security obligations. Provider retention practices may vary. If you prefer to avoid any cloud processing, you can choose not to use the chat, tarot, horoscope, or dream interpretation features.

4. Storage and security

  • Most data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes local data.
  • We do not run a public cloud database for AURA-BREE at this time. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated before the change takes effect.
  • We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Sharing

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We share data only with service providers that help operate the app, such as AI processing and crash reporting, and only for the limited purpose of providing the service.
  • We may disclose information if required by applicable law or to protect the rights, safety, or integrity of users and the service.

6. Retention

Local data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app. Transient data sent to our processors is retained only as long as necessary to provide the feature and meet security and legal obligations described by those processors.

7. Your choices

  • You can delete mood history, chat messages, tarot logs, and dream logs in the app where available or by uninstalling the app.
  • You may contact us to request deletion of any server side support communications you have sent to us.
  • You can opt out of AI features by not using them. Core navigation remains available.

8. Children

AURA-BREE is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Wellness notice

AURA-BREE provides wellness support and reflective tools. It is not a medical device and does not provide professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. For emergencies, call local emergency services or a crisis hotline.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The Last updated date at the top will reflect the most recent version. Material changes will be highlighted in the app or on this page.
