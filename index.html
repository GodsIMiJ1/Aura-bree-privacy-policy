About AURA-BREE

AURA-BREE is your personal therapeutic AI companion. The app offers a calm, judgment free space to check in with yourself, get guidance, and build resilience. Everything is designed for privacy, clarity, and control.

Wellness note: AURA-BREE supports wellbeing but is not a medical device and does not provide professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Features

  • Mood tracking - log mood and notes, then review trends in Progress.
  • AI chat support - talk through what you are feeling. Conversations are processed only to provide responses.
  • Tarot card reader - draw cards for Past, Present, and Future to guide reflection.
  • Daily horoscope - receive a brief outlook to set your vibe for the day.
  • Dream interpreter - log a dream and explore symbolic meanings for personal insight.
  • Emergency toolkit - quick access to crisis numbers and a guided 4-4-6 breathing pattern for grounding.

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how GodsIMiJ AI Solutions collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you use the AURA-BREE Android application. By using the app you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

  • User provided content - mood entries, notes, chat messages, tarot selections, dream logs, horoscope preferences, and optional contact details if you email us.
  • Device and diagnostics - device model, operating system version, app version, crash reports, performance logs, and coarse analytics such as feature usage counts.
  • Usage data - actions within the app such as opening features, number of sessions, and basic interaction telemetry. This is aggregated or pseudonymous.

We do not require your legal name, address, or phone number to use the app. You may choose to provide contact details for support.

2. How we use information

  • Provide and maintain core features including chat responses, mood tracking, tarot reader, horoscope, and dream interpretation.
  • Protect the app from fraud, abuse, and security threats, and troubleshoot crashes or bugs.
  • Analyze aggregated usage to improve reliability, accessibility, and user experience.
  • Respond to support requests that you initiate.

3. AI processing

When you use AI features, the text you enter is transmitted to a third party AI service provider acting as our processor. Inputs are used only to generate a response and to protect the service from abuse. Prompts are sent over HTTPS. Providers are required to handle data under confidentiality and security obligations. Provider retention practices may vary. If you prefer to avoid any cloud processing, do not use the chat, tarot, horoscope, or dream interpretation features.

4. Storage and security

  • Most data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes local data.
  • We do not operate a public cloud database for AURA-BREE at this time. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated before the change takes effect.
  • We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of information.

5. Sharing

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We share data only with service providers that help operate the app, such as AI processing and crash reporting, and only for the limited purpose of providing the service.
  • We may disclose information if required by applicable law or to protect the rights, safety, or integrity of users and the service.

6. Retention

Local data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app. Transient data sent to our processors is retained only as long as necessary to provide the feature and meet security and legal obligations described by those processors.

7. International transfers
